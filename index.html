Our Mission

We believe fitness should be accessible, sustainable, and empowering. IronCore exists to help members build strength, confidence, and lifelong habits—without intimidation or gimmicks.

Whether you are training for your first 5K or your next powerlifting meet, our coaches meet you where you are and push you toward where you want to be.

Core Values

  • 01

    Community First

    Every member matters. We celebrate progress together—from first pull-up to personal records.

  • 02

    Evidence-Based Training

    Programs rooted in sports science, not trends. Safe progressions and smart recovery built in.

  • 03

    Inclusive Environment

    All bodies, all backgrounds, all experience levels. Judgment-free zones in every corner of the gym.

  • 04

    Accountability

    Coaches who know your name, check-ins that matter, and goals you actually track.

Programs & Amenities

  • Strength & Conditioning — barbell, kettlebell, and functional circuits
  • Group Classes — HIIT, yoga, spin, and boxing (40+ weekly sessions)
  • Personal Training — 1-on-1 and small-group coaching packages
  • Recovery Zone — sauna, cold plunge, and mobility stations
  • Nutrition Coaching — monthly workshops and optional meal planning
  • Junior Athletes — ages 12–17 strength fundamentals program
